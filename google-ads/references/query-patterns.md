# query-patterns.md

## Purpose
Interpret search terms as signals, not just strings.

## Pattern families
- Transactional / action-oriented modifiers
- Comparison modifiers
- Education / research modifiers
- Problem-aware pain modifiers
- Support / navigation modifiers
- Employment modifiers
- Free / bargain modifiers
- Geography modifiers
- Brand and competitor modifiers

## Action labels
- Keep
- Scale
- Isolate
- Exclude
- Watch

## Copy mining rule
When high-performing queries repeat modifiers or phrases, extract them for:
- RSA headlines
- RSA descriptions
- LP headlines
- test ideas
