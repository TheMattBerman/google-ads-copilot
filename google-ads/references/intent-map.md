# intent-map.md

## What an Intent Map is
An Intent Map is a durable model of the different search intents inside a Google Ads account.

It tracks:
- what kinds of searches are valuable
- which ones are noisy
- which ones are dangerous to mix
- what structural and messaging decisions follow from that

## Core intent classes
- Buyer intent
- Comparison intent
- Research intent
- Existing-customer / support intent
- Job-seeker intent
- Freebie / low-commercial intent
- Competitor intent
- Branded intent

## Output expectation
A good Intent Map should answer:
- what buyers look like in this account
- what junk looks like in this account
- what comparisons matter
- what language repeats among winners
- what traffic types should never share the same bucket
- what changes should happen next
