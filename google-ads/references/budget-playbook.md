# budget-playbook.md

## Core principle
Budget decisions should follow signal quality, not impatience.

Do not scale because something looks exciting for one day.
Scale when the traffic quality, conversion quality, and structural clarity support it.

## Budget questions
- Is the campaign buying the right intent?
- Is conversion tracking trustworthy enough to justify scale?
- Is the campaign constrained by budget or by poor traffic quality?
- Would more budget amplify signal or amplify waste?
- Is this a scale decision or a structure-cleanup decision pretending to be scale?

## Typical actions
- protect budget on proven high-intent buckets
- restrict budget on mixed or waste-heavy buckets
- move budget from noisy generic traffic to cleaner intent buckets
- avoid scaling unstable buckets too aggressively

## Output expectation
- what deserves more budget
- what deserves less budget
- what should be fixed before scaling
- confidence level
