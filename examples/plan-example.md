# Example: Account Plan

> Starting from scratch or restructuring. The agent designs campaign architecture around intent separation, not keyword grouping.

## What the account is telling us
The budget is too small to support a fancy structure, but large enough to support clean separation between brand, non-brand buyer intent, and comparison traffic.

## What matters most
- Keep the initial structure simple.
- Separate meaning early so reporting stays clean.
- Avoid buying educational traffic until the core buyer buckets are stable.

## Proposed campaign structure
- Brand Search
- Non-Brand Buyer Intent
- Comparison / Alternatives

## Proposed ad group logic
- group by commercial meaning, not just shared root words
- keep each ad group close to one landing page promise

## Exclusion logic
- jobs/careers cluster in shared negatives
- support/login terms excluded from acquisition
- educational modifiers excluded from BOF launch setup

## Message strategy by bucket
- Brand: trust + easiest next step
- Buyer: direct response around demo/pricing/pain relief
- Comparison: proof + differentiation

## Budget posture
- protect brand
- bias spend toward clean buyer intent
- keep comparison traffic controlled until it proves itself
